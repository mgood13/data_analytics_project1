1. Dataset- https://www.kaggle.com/open-source-sports/baseball-databank
   1. Baseball Databank is a compilation of historical baseball data in a
convenient, tidy format, distributed under Open Data terms.
2. Questions-
   1. Teams that spend more on players win more 
   2. Does birth month impact skill
   3. Batting percentages for right hand versus left hand 
   4. Do teams that win more have different aspects of that matter less? (aka averages of hitting, batting  fielding)
   5. Over time which team does better 
3. Our project is to uncover patterns in baseball history. We are doing this by evaluating trends in skill vs biological factors and winning percentages vs other factors.
